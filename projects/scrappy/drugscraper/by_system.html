From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category reflects the organization of Anatomical Therapeutic Chemical Classification System.
Generally, drugs outlined within the ATC Classification System should be included in this category.
Please see WP:PHARM:CAT for more information.
Wikimedia Commons has media related to Drugs by target organ system.
